Nachos

Description

Delicious cheesy, crunchy, and savory snack sure to please even the most discerning of party guests!

Ingredients

  • Corn Chips
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Salsa
  • Olives
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Add the chips to a dish for the oven
  2. Cover the chips liberally with Cheese
  3. Bake at 400 degrees until the cheese is melted to your taste
  4. Cook the meat to your taste on the stove top
  5. Add the meat to the top of the melted cheese covered chips
  6. Add the rest of the ingredients to your taste
