Cheeseburger

Description

Delicious cheesy, savory dinner sure to please even the most discerning of dinner guests!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Mayonaise
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Cook the meat to your taste on the stove to
  2. Add sliced cheese to the top of the meat while it is still hot
  3. Toast bun
  4. Spread mayonaise on the bun to your taste
  5. Add Meat, tomato, and pickles to the bun
